Welcome to MALIBU v1.0.0

Thank you for choosing MALIBU, the professional multi-slope low-pass filter plugin by Solmar Labs.

What's Included

  • Audio Unit (AU) format for Logic Pro, GarageBand, and other AU-compatible DAWs
  • VST3 format for Pro Tools, Ableton Live, Reaper, and other VST3-compatible DAWs
  • Complete user documentation and quick start guide

System Requirements

  • macOS 10.14 (Mojave) or later
  • Intel 64-bit or Apple Silicon Mac
  • 4 GB RAM minimum (8 GB recommended)
  • 50 MB available disk space

Important: After installation, please restart your DAW and rescan for new plugins to ensure MALIBU appears in your plugin list.
